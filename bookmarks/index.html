List of interesting links with brief introductions.

Previously available at “Links” page (now empty).

Web Sustainability Guidelines🔗

Multi-faceted and comprehensive guide to making sustainable web sites/products, with links to research and examples.

Godot Engine Docs (Getting Started)🔗

Explanations of basic godot concepts, as well as more detailed walkthroughs and a full reference for nodes, functions, variables, best practices, and general topics.

NixOS & Flakes Book🔗

An (unofficial) introduction to Nix, which includes flakes (unlike official docs), and seems more beginner friendly and comprehensive than the official docs. Aims to include everything you might need.

Soatok/Dhole Moments🔗

Great writing on security, but also posts on general things, and a good series on getting jobs (in tech; called “Furward Momentum”). The about page has a list of “top posts”. Filippo Valsorda is also a good resource for cryptography and security.

lobste.rs🔗

Well-moderated tech forum.

Marginalia Search🔗

Search engine that tries to be indie, relevant, and free of corporate marketing.

CL Cookbook🔗

Handy articles and references for getting stuff done in Common Lisp.

Lisp Journey/Vindarel🔗

Website by Vindarel — big contributor to Common Lisp cookbook and community.

Ye Olde Blogroll🔗

Short and curated list of personal and indie blogs.

Sourcehut🔗

Indie (ish?) and FOSS source forge with interesting UI/UX and more, started by drewdevault and company.

Drew Devault🔗

Articles on FOSS software and community, and tech in general.

Seirdy🔗

Articles on accessibility, security, privacy, FOSS. Includes a good one on accessibility that’s inspired a fair bit of my UI/UX designs, and some of the design for the current website (to be fair, the article is a compilation of various other sources).

DanLuu🔗

Well thought out posts on various topics, especially tech and personal productivity/improvement.

Farnam Street Blog🔗

Articles on improving yourself.
