How does it work?ğŸ”—

A feed is a simple link that you put in a feed reader app, usually by copying it.

Feed readers have varying degrees of functionality. At a base level, they should be able to list posts from all the people that you follow, with a link to the post on their website.

Additional features include viewing the post in the app, which is fairly common, searching all feeds and posts, grouping them into categories, receiving notifications on updates, and more.
