Link to feed

For the uninitiated, web feeds are a mechanism for sharing updates, because websites don’t really have a specific structure. It’s a special link (mine is above) that goes into a feed reader application, of which there are several. Here’s a few:

  • RSS is Awesome

    If you tend to consume content on a single device, use this for a free and simple reader with no sign-up and no ads.

  • Feed Reader

    This is similar to the above service, but with a required account for syncing across multiple devices.

  • Feedly

    Feedly is quite big and well known, with apps for multiple platforms, at the cost of ads, sponsored content, and other limitations for free accounts.

Ironically, I hammered out the feed after I read a post by someone who doesn’t like feeds, despite not caring for them myself, just because I could.

I even went through the effort of styling it with XSL, which was the main draw for me, honestly.
