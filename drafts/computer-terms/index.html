For example, if you your shirt or blouse is not in your almirah, you will have too look everywhere, and you will be late for work.

“Windows (OS)”

The main “app” inside which other apps and things open.

Windows (OS) has the taskbar, desktop, window close buttons, start menu, settings and more.

OS means operating system, which means the “app” inside which other apps open, and where files and folders exist. Other operating systems include Android, which is on your phone, and Linux, macOS, and iOS.
