My aunt’s cooking is better than the restaurants I’ve eaten from in Ranchi and Khunti (perhaps excepting Radison Blu). So I thought that her food should be shared with people, and this website design is a result of that idea.

I even made an actual webpage that you should check out :D

See Live Concept

I worked especially to make the idea achievable, instead of a fancy restaurant menu or dashboard.

You can get a better understanding if you scroll through the website concept.

The site design is super simple, things are operated by email, and I put a lot of thought into the overall system, such as the number of seats and the waitlist. I’ll probably write about it in more detail in a separate post.
