Movin' Bites Packaging and Branding

Biscuits are often eaten on the go, like when you need to catch your bus and you don't have the time to eat your breakfast, but then they crumble and make a complete mess.

Movin' Bites is here to solve that.

Goals

- Prevent biscuits from crumbling
- Prevent spilling
- Enable storage
  - Most packs can't hold biscuits if you decide to keep some for later
- Explore new shapes and structures
  - Differentiate from competition
- Depict "on-the-go" and classic salted flavour

Exploration

Packaging

Triangles and hexagons are the strongest shapes in nature. For example, the inside of cardboard is made up of several triangles.

I finalised the hexagon box because it was made of one piece, which made it easy to assemble, and round biscuits fit inside it better.

Branding

I decided to keep the brand friendly and fun with vibrant colours and rounded shapes, while still expressing "on-the-go".

Result

The final prototype was made by printing the design on vinyl sheets and pasting the sheets onto a structure made with file-card.
